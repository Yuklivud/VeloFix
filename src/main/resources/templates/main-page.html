About Us

Welcome to VeloFIX – your trusted bicycle service center! We specialize in high-quality warranty repairs for bicycles, ensuring that your ride stays in top condition for years to come. But we don’t stop there! We also offer paid repair and maintenance services, helping cyclists keep their bikes in perfect shape even beyond the warranty period.

Our team of experienced mechanics is dedicated to diagnosing and fixing all types of issues, from minor adjustments to major overhauls. Whether you need a quick tune-up, brake adjustments, wheel truing, or a complete drivetrain replacement, we've got you covered.

At VeloFIX, we value quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. We use only top-grade spare parts and the latest repair techniques to ensure your bike runs smoothly and safely. Whether you're a professional cyclist or a casual rider, we’re here to provide the best service possible.

Services

At VeloFIX, we offer a full range of bicycle repair and maintenance services to keep your bike in perfect condition. Whether you need a warranty repair or a paid service, our team of skilled mechanics is ready to help.

Warranty Repairs

If your bike is still under warranty, we provide free diagnostics and repairs for manufacturer defects. Our specialists carefully inspect your bike, replace faulty parts, and ensure everything works as intended.

Paid Repair & Maintenance

For bicycles outside the warranty period or for issues not covered by the warranty, we offer professional repair and maintenance services to ensure your bike performs at its best.
